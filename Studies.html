Studies

After browsing my blog a bit, you may have noticed that I am studying Networks and Telecommunications. I made this choice because my interest in computers and everything related to it was growing, I wanted to learn more about the functioning of machines and software.

As I have a friend who has already done this type of study I asked her for advice regarding the training and she convinced me that this was the closest thing to what I was looking for.

My choice having fallen on Brittany for its attraction on telecommunications technologies I therefore applied to several places and Lannion was my final choice. I am happy to be here, I have met a lot of people and the training is great! Even though I miss my family, here I feel good in a family town.
